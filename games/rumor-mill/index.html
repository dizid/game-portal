Rumor Mill

Seed a rumor on one node (click). Each tick, believers spread your rumor — but mutations degrade similarity. After 10 ticks a counter-rumor appears.

Abilities: Boost (re-inject original), Discredit (reduce credulity), Bridge (connect 2 distant nodes). Each has 8-tick cooldown. 30 ticks total.

Score = % of network believing your rumor variant (>50% similarity).
